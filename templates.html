Why use Mitti proposal templates?

Indian freelancers have unique requirements when sending proposals and contracts. Unlike Western templates, Indian business documents must account for:

  • GST compliance: CGST + SGST for intra-state services, IGST for inter-state, and zero-rated for exports
  • SAC codes: Service Accounting Codes required on B2B invoices above ₹20L turnover
  • TDS Section 194J: Professional fees subject to 10% TDS deduction by the client
  • Indian Contract Act 1872: All templates use governing law clauses appropriate for Indian jurisdiction
  • Payment terms: Standard Indian practice (50% advance, balance on delivery)

All Mitti templates are generated fresh by AI, so they're never stale, always personalised, and ready to send within minutes. Unlike static Word templates, Mitti understands your project details and writes proposals that actually win clients.

Frequently asked questions

Are these templates legally valid in India?
The templates are designed for Indian law but are AI-generated starting points. For high-value contracts (above ₹5L), we recommend having a lawyer review them. For standard freelance proposals and agreements under ₹5L, they're appropriate as-is.

Do I need a GST registration to use these?
No. The GST fields are optional. If your turnover is below the ₹20L threshold, you can leave GST fields blank and the template will generate a non-GST document.

Can I use these templates for free?
Yes. Free accounts get 3 AI-generated documents per month. The invoice generator is always free and unlimited.
