If your client is in the same state as you, GST splits into CGST + SGST (half each). Different state = IGST (full rate). Mitti detects this automatically based on the addresses you enter.
Service Accounting Code — required on all B2B GST invoices above ₹20L turnover. Mitti suggests the right one for your service type automatically.
If a company pays you for professional services, they must deduct 10% TDS before paying you. Mitti calculates exactly what they owe you and includes the clause in your document.
The documents are strong starting points written for Indian law. We recommend having a lawyer review contracts above ₹5 lakh.
