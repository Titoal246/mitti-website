Setting Client Expectations on Results

The biggest source of conflict in social media retainers is clients expecting guaranteed follower growth or viral reach. Address this head-on in your proposal and onboarding call:

  • Commit to inputs, not outputs. You can guarantee 20 posts per month, daily community management, and a monthly report — you cannot guarantee 1,000 new followers per month.
  • Set a 90-day benchmark. Tell clients that meaningful organic growth typically takes 3–6 months of consistent content. Month 1 is foundation-building.
  • Separate organic from paid. Organic social media grows slowly. If the client expects rapid growth, they need a paid media budget — which is a separate scope item.

GST note: Social media management services fall under SAC code 998361 (Advertising and related services). GST at 18% applies on your management fee if you're registered. Ad spend passed through to Meta/Google is not your supply — don't charge GST on money you're collecting and forwarding to the platform.
