Frequently asked questions
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Mitti works entirely in your browser. Your documents are saved locally on your device. Upgrading to Pro ties your subscription to your email address and device fingerprint — no password to remember.
What happens after 3 free documents?
Your counter resets every month on the 1st. You can also earn extra free documents by referring Mitti to other freelancers — each successful referral gives you +1 document. Or upgrade to Pro for unlimited access.
Is the payment secure? What methods are accepted?
Payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), all debit/credit cards, and net banking. There is no auto-renewal — each subscription period must be renewed manually.
Are the documents legally valid in India?
The documents Mitti generates are comprehensive and based on Indian law (Indian Contract Act 1872, IT Act 2000, GST Act 2017). They cover all standard clauses Indian freelancers need. However, for large-value contracts or unusual situations, we recommend having a lawyer review. Mitti is a starting point, not a substitute for legal advice.
How does the GST calculation work?
Mitti automatically detects whether your transaction is intra-state (CGST + SGST) or inter-state (IGST) based on your state and client's state. For overseas clients, it applies zero-rating under LUT. SAC codes are auto-suggested based on your service type. This is built-in — no manual configuration needed.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes. Since there is no auto-renewal, your Pro access simply expires at the end of your billing month. You don't need to cancel anything — just don't renew. Your saved documents remain accessible in My Docs even after expiry.
I'm an agency with a team. What's the Agency plan?
The Agency plan at ₹799/month is designed for studios and agencies. It includes everything in Pro, plus support for up to 5 team members and custom branding on generated documents. Contact hello@mitti.in to set up team access.
